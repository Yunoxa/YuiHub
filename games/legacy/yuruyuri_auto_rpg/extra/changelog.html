Version 1.0905.1

- A message is now displayed in the log upon saving and loading.
- Added a new "beach" dungeon.
- Weapon qualities are now coloured to give them more emphasis.
- Pickaxes now also mine ores under their highest value ore.
- Each character now has a class. Classes have their own weapons which have unique stats and graphics. Each class also has their own special abilities:
1) Yui begins with a +1 weapon kill multiplier, and gains twice as much weapon kill multiplier every 50 weapon levels.
2) Kyoko has increased overall XP gain and can recieve "Rum Raisin" from enemies, increasing her total power upon consumption
3) Akari does increased damage against bully type enemies, and can perform rescue operations to earn extra money. She also gains double XP and money from these sources every 50 levels. However she sometimes may lose her presence and another character will recieve her XP and Money reward.
4) Chinatsu has a huge variety of different spells she can use at random, providing various bonuses and effects.
- A class section has been added to each character panel.
- New gold weapons and a pickaxe have been added.
- Bully tags have been added to random enemies, increasing their damage, but only having an increased XP and Money output for the Ranger class.
- Pickaxe prices slightly increased.
- Location images added, made from a mix of AI generated art from artbreeder and Yuru Yuri screenshots.
- Enemy grouping system implemented to reduce clutter caused by kill multipliers.
- Kill multipliers now give a random multiplier between 1 and the maximum per attack, rather than the full multiplier every time.
- A few new enemies added to dungeons.
- Characters no longer loiter in the store when they have nothing else to do.

Version 1.0703.1

- The enemy system has been completely redone. Enemies now spawn with different tags which determine their XP value and also power.
- Dungeons now provide a multiplier to enemies power and XP output. Earlier dungeons have lower multipliers, but harder dungeons have increased multipliers, providing more XP but also having increased difficulty.
- Weapons have been totally overhauled. They can no longer be purchased in the shop, and are now earned randomly as drops from enemies.
- Weapons now have multiple random attributes, such as Material, Power Type, Quality, and level.
- A kill multiplier has been introduced. Every 50 weapon levels a weapon will add one to the kill multiplier, which allows a character to perform multiple enemy attacks at once.
- Mining level now increases ore sell prices by Ore price * Mining Level.
- Level and mining level now display a notification in the log once they are levelled up. They're also coloured for visibility!

Version 1.0629.1

- Added in graphics for pickaxes and swords!
- Mining level added, which increases based on the amount of ore mined.
- Number of ores mined is now counted, along with the amount required for the next level.
- Made minor code optimisations.
- Trash drops are now all worth 1 * sell multiplier.
- Trash drops are now tagged with [TRASH].
- Made the log less wide.

Version 1.0424.1

- The failed defeats of each character are now tracked.
- Fixed characters gaining 0.1* the money they're supposed to.
- Dungeon keys added.

- All characters begin with a wood sword that provides 25 weapon damage.
- The money required for the next pickaxe, next dungeon key, and next weapon is now displayed in the store stats section.

Version 1.0418.1

- Fixed level up power gain multiplying based on the current power, making the characters insanely op. Character power now increases by the characters base power * (character level * character power level up multiplier).
- Fixed power being NaN on loading saves from old versions.
