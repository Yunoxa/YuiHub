Ayano Sugiura

Ayano is the student council vice president. She's hard working and has a strong tsundere personality. She also has a crush on Toshinou Kyouko from the Amusement Club.

Chitose Ikeda

Chitose is a member of the student council and close friend of Ayano. She's very kind, but also dirty minded as she constantly fantasises about Ayano and Kyouko.

Sakurako Oomuro

Sakurako is a member of the student council. She's rather idiotic but very lively and energetic, constantly getting into conflict with Himawari.

Himawari Furutani

Himawari is a member of the student council. She's quite mature, but often ends up in conflict with Sakurako.
