Yui Funami

Yui Funami is one of the main characters in Yuru Yuri and a member of the Amusement club. She bears a mature personality and often retorts, especially when it comes to Kyoko.

Kyouko Toshinou

Kyouko Toshinou is one of the main characters in Yuru Yuri and a member of the Amusement club. She has a very wild personality and brings a lively atmosphere to the group.

Akari Akaza

Akari Akaza is the supposed protagonist Yuru Yuri and a member of the Amusement club. Despite being initially set up as the protagonist her severe lack of presence results in her often being forgotten.

Chinatsu Yoshikawa

Chinatsu is one of the main characters in Yuru Yuri and a member of the Amusement club. She is deeply in love with Yui, constantly obsessing over her. Despite her cute personality she's also rather freaky.
